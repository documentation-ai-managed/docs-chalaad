## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">

Before starting, ensure you have:
- A valid email address for account creation
- Internet access on your preferred device (desktop, tablet, or mobile)
- Basic information ready, such as your name and shipping address for orders

</Callout>

## Create Your CHALAAD Account

Follow these steps to sign up in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Website" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to [https://chalaad.com](https://chalaad.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Registration" icon="user-plus">
    Click the `Sign Up` button in the top-right corner.
    
    Fill in your details:
    
    | Field       | Example                  |
    |-------------|--------------------------|
    | Email       | you@example.com          |
    | Password    | (Choose a strong one)    |
    | Full Name   | John Doe                 |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification email from CHALAAD and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the site, enter your credentials, and access your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
