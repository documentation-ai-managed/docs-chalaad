---
title: Smart Device Not Connecting
description: Fixing connectivity issues for CHALAAD smart home devices.
---

## Problem

- Device not appearing in the CHALAAD app
- "Connection failed" error during setup

## Causes

- Device not in pairing mode
- Wi-Fi signal too weak
- Wrong Wi-Fi credentials

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check pairing mode">
    Ensure the device is powered on and indicator light is blinking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Wi-Fi details">
    Double-check your Wi-Fi password and connect to a 2.4GHz network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move closer to router">
    Place the device near your router during setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>
