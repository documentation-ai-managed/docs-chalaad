## Smart Switch Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Turn off power">
    Switch off the power at the main circuit breaker.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove old switch">
    Unscrew the faceplate, disconnect wires, and remove the existing switch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect new switch wires">
    Match CHALAAD smart switch wires (line, load, neutral, ground). Tighten all screws.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mount and power on">
    Attach the new faceplate, restore power, and confirm LED status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pair with app">
    Open CHALAAD app, add device, and follow on-screen setup steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>
